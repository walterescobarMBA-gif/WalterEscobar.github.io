Notice of Transmittal

This audit is formally transmitted to Alliance for Housing and Healing (APLA Health) and associated program administrators for review and response.

Requested action includes a written response addressing each finding and identification of any corrective measures taken or planned.

Status

Q3–Q4 2025 audit completed and transmitted. No documented sponsor response or corrective action received.

Q3–Q4 2025 Audit

The Q3–Q4 2025 audit documents sponsor-level administrative and compliance failures, including lack of response to formal findings, defective grievance routing, and absence of corrective action following notice.

Download Q3–Q4 2025 Audit PDF

Key Findings

  • Failure to provide written response to formal audit transmittal
  • Grievance pathway directed to an entity without jurisdiction
  • No independent oversight triggered following escalation
  • No documented corrective action following formal notice

Q1–Q2 2026 Continuation Review

The Q1–Q2 2026 audit period is currently ongoing and will evaluate whether previously identified deficiencies were remediated following formal notice.

The continuation review will assess sponsor response, corrective action, and the effectiveness of grievance and oversight mechanisms.

Conclusion

The audit establishes that identified deficiencies were formally documented, transmitted, and not addressed. The absence of response and the failure of the grievance pathway to provide independent review reflect a breakdown in administrative controls and accountability mechanisms within a publicly funded housing program.
