HOPWA Sponsor Compliance Audit

Status: Submitted, No Response Received

Summary

This audit documents systemic failures in program administration, including lack of response to formal findings, defective grievance routing, and continued housing instability following notice.

Key Findings

  • Failure to respond to formal audit transmittal
  • Grievance system misdirects participants to non-jurisdictional entities
  • No independent oversight triggered after escalation
  • Continued instability during non-response period

Conclusion

The system failed, was notified, and failed again. This reflects a breakdown in accountability mechanisms within a publicly funded housing program.
