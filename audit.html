HOPWA Sponsor Compliance Audit

Participant-Prepared Audit | Accountability and Oversight Review

Status

Formal audit submitted. No written response received.

Summary

This audit evaluates systemic failures in housing program administration, including lack of response to formal findings, defective grievance routing, and continued instability following notice.

Key Findings

  • Failure to respond to formal audit transmittal
  • Grievance system directs participants to non-jurisdictional entities
  • No independent oversight triggered after escalation
  • Continued instability during unresolved audit period

Timeline

  • January 2026: Audit formally transmitted with response deadline
  • January 26, 2026: Deadline passed with no response
  • Late January 2026: Grievance filed using program instructions
  • February 2026: County declined jurisdiction and redirected back to sponsor
  • Ongoing: No corrective action or oversight intervention

Conclusion

The system failed, was notified, and failed again. This reflects a breakdown in accountability mechanisms within a publicly funded housing program.
