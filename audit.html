Status

Q3–Q4 2025 audit completed and published. Q1–Q2 2026 continuation audit currently in progress.

Q3–Q4 2025 Audit

The Q3–Q4 2025 audit documents sponsor-level administrative and compliance failures, including lack of response to formal findings, defective grievance routing, and absence of corrective action following notice.

Download Q3–Q4 2025 Audit PDF

Key Findings (Q3–Q4 2025)

  • Failure to provide written response to formal audit transmittal
  • Grievance pathway directed to an entity without jurisdiction
  • No independent oversight triggered following escalation
  • No documented corrective action following formal notice

Q1–Q2 2026 Continuation Review

The Q1–Q2 2026 audit period is currently ongoing. This continuation review will assess whether previously identified deficiencies were remediated following formal notice.

The continuation audit will evaluate:

  • Whether a sponsor response was provided following the Q3–Q4 audit
  • Whether corrective actions were implemented
  • Whether grievance pathways function as represented
  • Whether oversight mechanisms were triggered following escalation

The Q1–Q2 audit will be published after the close of the review period.

Conclusion

The Q3–Q4 audit establishes that deficiencies were formally documented and transmitted without documented response or corrective action. The continuation review will determine whether these conditions persisted following notice.
