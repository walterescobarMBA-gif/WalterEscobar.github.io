About This Work

This site presents participant-prepared documentation concerning HOPWA program administration, housing stability, grievance processes, and oversight accountability.

The purpose is to preserve a clear public record, identify systemic failures, and support policy-level review.

This is not just a housing dispute. It is a documented breakdown in process, communication, oversight, and participant protection.

HOPWA Audit Documentation

The Q3–Q4 2025 audit documents sponsor-level deficiencies, including lack of transition planning, inconsistent communication, displacement risk, and absence of corrective action following notice.
